Speakers

Who's doing the talking

Troy Hunt

Pluralsight Author, Microsoft Regional Director and Most Valued Professional (MVP).

Pluralsight Author, Microsoft Regional Director and Most Valued Professional (MVP) specialising in online security and cloud development. Regular speaker at conferences around the world and run workshops on how to build more secure software within organisations. Also the creator of the data breach aggregation service known as "Have I been pwned".

Dan Toomey

Enterprise Integration Consultant at Mexia

Dan Toomey is a Senior Consultant for Mexia and a Microsoft Certified Azure Developer (MCPD), as well as a Microsoft Certified Technical Specialist (MCTS) in BizTalk Server, a Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT), and a Pluralsight author. During his 13+ years of experience in the Australian IT industry, Dan has helped a number of large private and government organisation solve their enterprise integration problems using Microsoft technologies. An active community blogger and speaker, Dan founded the Brisbane BizTalk User Group in 2005 and is now a chief organiser of the Brisbane Azure User Group.

Todd Whitehead

Cloud Solution Architect at Microsoft

Todd Whitehead has a proven track record in leading complex Enterprise IT projects. The combination of solid technical skills, project management experience and team leadership ensure he can deliver whatever a client requires to make their project a success. His core focus is delivering enterprise web applications using Agile development methodologies and Microsoft’s’ .Net technology stack. In this capacity he has helped clients ranging from small start-ups to large corporations realize better business outcomes by leveraging Agile principles and practices.
