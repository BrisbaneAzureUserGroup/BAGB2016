Speakers

Who's doing the talking

Jeff Alexander

IT Pro Evangelist at Microsoft.

Jeff Alexander started at Microsoft in August 1987 as a Support Professional focusing on operating systems and desktop applications for Enterprise Customers. Since then he spent 6 years in Microsoft’s internal IT team as a Datacenter manager; one of his biggest projects being a server consolidation of the Sydney site to Singapore. Jeff Alexander now focuses his passion for Infrastructure and management technologies as an IT Pro Evangelist promoting and enabling the community of IT Professionals through vehicles as Technet events; blogging, the TechNet Web Site , User Groups, MVP's, and online webcasts.

Alessandro Cardoso

Technology Strategist | National Lead Cloud, Identity and Security at RXP Services Limited

With 24 plus years of success in IT industry, I thrive in an environment where there is a need to take complex business problems, delivering technology enabled transformational business outcomes with resource and time constraints, transforming business strategy into technology utilising existing and innovative technologies.

Daniel Toomey

Integration Architect, Mexia

Dan Toomey is a Consultant for Mexia and a Microsoft Certified Azure Developer (MCPD), as well as a Microsoft Certified Technical Specialist (MCTS) in BizTalk Server, a Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT), and a Pluralsight author. During his 13+ years of experience in the Australian IT industry, Dan has helped a number of large private and government organisation solve their enterprise integration problems using Microsoft technologies. He founded the Brisbane BizTalk User Group in 2005 and has presented at many of its sessions since then. He’s also presented for the Brisbane Azure User Group (for which he is now an organiser) and at numerous Barcamps in Brisbane and on the Gold Coast.

Steve van Bodegraven.

Premier Field Engineer at Microsoft.

As a Microsoft Premier Field Engineer, Steve helps developers and software development teams increase their productivity on the Microsoft development platform, including Application Lifecycle Management, DevOps, Mobility, Modern Apps and Predictive Analytics.
